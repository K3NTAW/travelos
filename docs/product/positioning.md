# Positioning

## Primary ICP
- Frequent business travelers and their team coordinators/assistants.

## Core Promise
- Reduce travel stress and decision latency with one trusted command center.

## Differentiator
- Dual-track live status (official + predicted), explainable changes, and team coordination in one workflow.

## Non-goals (v1)
- Competing as a full OTA.
- Broad consumer leisure-first scope.
