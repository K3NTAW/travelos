# Personas

## Traveler (Business Flyer)
- Needs: confidence, proactive alerts, and low-friction itinerary changes.
- Success metric: fewer missed updates and faster airport decisions.

## Coordinator (Assistant/Ops)
- Needs: visibility across many travelers, risk sorting, and role-safe controls.
- Success metric: lower disruption handling time and fewer manual follow-ups.

## Admin (Finance/Travel Ops)
- Needs: policy controls, auditability, and spend visibility.
- Success metric: compliance confidence and lower travel ops overhead.
